# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We, as members of the MWECAU ICT Club, pledge to make participation in our community a respectful, harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We pledge to act in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming, diverse, and healthy community.

## Our Standards

### Expected Behavior

✅ **Be Respectful**: Treat everyone with respect and kindness  
✅ **Be Collaborative**: Work together and help each other learn  
✅ **Be Professional**: Use welcoming and inclusive language  
✅ **Be Constructive**: Give and receive feedback gracefully  
✅ **Be Patient**: Remember that everyone is learning  
✅ **Give Credit**: Acknowledge others' contributions  

### Unacceptable Behavior

❌ Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination of any kind  
❌ Offensive, insulting, or derogatory comments  
❌ Personal or political attacks  
❌ Publishing others' private information without permission  
❌ Trolling, spam, or disruptive behavior  
❌ Plagiarism or claiming others' work as your own  
❌ Any conduct that could reasonably be considered inappropriate  

## Responsibilities

### Club Members

- Follow this Code of Conduct in all club activities
- Report violations to club leadership
- Support a positive and inclusive environment

### Club Leadership

- Enforce this Code of Conduct fairly and consistently
- Address reported violations promptly and confidentially
- Take appropriate corrective action when necessary
- Lead by example in upholding these standards

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all club spaces, including:

- GitHub repositories and discussions
- Club meetings and events
- Social media and online platforms
- Email and messaging groups
- Any public space where you represent the club

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior:

1. **Report to Leadership**:
   - Club President
   - Club Secretary
   - Technical Team Lead
   - Any club patron/advisor

2. **Contact Methods**:
   - In person at club meetings
   - Email: mwecauictclub@gmail.com
   - Private message on WhatsApp group
   - Through university channels if needed

3. **What to Include**:
   - What happened
   - When and where it happened
   - Who was involved
   - Any supporting evidence (screenshots, links, etc.)

All reports will be handled with discretion and confidentiality.

## Enforcement

### Review Process

1. Leadership will review the report
2. Investigation will be conducted fairly
3. Appropriate action will be determined
4. All parties will be notified of the outcome

### Consequences

Depending on the severity, violations may result in:

1. **Warning**: Private written warning with clarification
2. **Temporary Suspension**: Temporary ban from club activities
3. **Permanent Ban**: Removal from the club and all activities
4. **University Action**: Report to university administration if serious

Leadership has the right and responsibility to remove, edit, or reject contributions, comments, and other interactions that violate this Code of Conduct.

## Appeal

If you disagree with an enforcement decision, you may appeal by:
- Contacting a different leadership member
- Writing to the club [patron/advisor](https://github.com/carmel26)
- Following university grievance procedures
